Our Story

Design Born from
Passion & Purpose

Founded in 2016 in the heart of Bangalore, Aura Space Interiors was born from a belief that great design transforms not just spaces — but the way people feel and live within them.

Our multidisciplinary team of 28 designers, architects, and craftspeople work collaboratively to create spaces that are uniquely yours — balancing beauty with function, luxury with liveability. From cosy apartments to sprawling villas and corporate offices, we've transformed over 250 spaces across Bangalore.

How We Work

Our Design
Process

A seamless journey from first conversation to final reveal — six steps to your dream space.

01
💬

Discovery Call

A free 45-minute consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle, space dimensions, and budget. We listen before we design.

02
📐

Concept & Moodboards

Our designers craft multiple concept directions with curated moodboards, colour palettes, material samples, and furniture references.

03
🖥️

3D Visualisation

Photorealistic 3D renders of every room so you see exactly what your space will look like before execution begins.

04
✍️

Design Sign-Off

Review, refine, and approve. We iterate until you're absolutely delighted. Detailed BOQ and transparent pricing provided.

05
🏗️

Execution

Our trusted craftsmen and project managers bring the design to life with precision, adhering strictly to timelines and quality standards.

06
🎉

Final Handover

A styled, fully complete space is handed over to you. We do a final walkthrough and provide a 1-year workmanship warranty.
